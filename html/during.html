More info
It's important that you do not eat after the midnight before your operation. Additionally, you cannot drink any fluids once you reach the hospital.
You should refrain from eating at least eight hours before your scheduled procedure to reduce side effects related to an aspiration. An aspiration is when stomach contents are expelled into the lungs and can cause significant damage.
